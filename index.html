What is Coffee?

Coffee is a beverage made from the roasted and ground seeds of African-derived tropical evergreen coffee bushes. Along with water and tea, coffee is one of the three most consumed beverages worldwide and one of the most successful global products. Although there are countless beverages that use coffee as their base, caffeine, a substance found in coffee, is primarily responsible for its energizing effects.
